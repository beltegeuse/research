Biography

I am an Assistant Professor at Ecole Supérieure de Technologie (ETS) inside the department of Information and Communications Technologies from September 2021 and member ob the Multimedia Lab.

I am actively looking to hire multiple Master/Ph.D. students who have a strong interest in computer graphics, particularly in rendering. If you are interested to work with me, please first read this page containing additional important information. Then, please send me an email and said you read this page and state your interest in computer graphics. If you have any question, don't hesitate to reach me out.

Adrien Gruson was a post-doc researcher at McGill University and The University of Tokyo under the supervision of Profs Nowrouzezahrai and Hachisuka. He was also a member of the Japanese French Laboratory of Informatics (JFLI). His research interests include physically-based rendering, using advance sampling techniques such Markov-chain Monte Carlo, gradient-based estimators.
