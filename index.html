Biography

Adrien Gruson is a post-doc researcher in Computer Graphics at the University of Tokyo. His research interests include physically based rendering (global illumination) with robust rendering techniques. He is currently in Prof. Toshiya Hachisuka’s lab. He is also a member of Japanese French Laboratory of Informatics (JFLI).
