Biography

Adrien Gruson is a post-doc researcher in Computer Graphics Group at the University of Tokyo (Prof. Toshiya Hachisuka’s lab). His research interests include physically-based rendering based on MCMC or gradient-domain approaches. He is also a member of Japanese French Laboratory of Informatics (JFLI).

Interests

  • Markov Chain Monte Carlo
  • Gradient-domain rendering
