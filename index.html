Biography

Adrien Gruson is a post-doc researcher at McGill University under the supervision of Prof. Nowrouzezahrai. Previously he was a post-doc at the University of Tokyo with Prof. Toshiya Hachisuka and was a member of Japanese French Laboratory of Informatics (JFLI). His research interests include physically-based rendering based on MCMC or gradient-domain approaches.

Interests

  • Markov Chain Monte Carlo
